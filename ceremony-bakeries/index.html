How It Works

Simple from start to celebration.

We keep the process clear so you can focus on the event — not the logistics.

1

Request

Send us your event date, size, and any ideas you have. We'll follow up within 24 hours to talk through the details.

2

Confirm

We finalize design, flavors, and timeline together. Once confirmed, your date is locked in and baking begins.

3

Pick Up or Deliver

Your order is ready on time — available for pickup at our Brooklyn location or coordinated delivery.
